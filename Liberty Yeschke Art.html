A Little About Me

I am a native Texan, and grew up mainly around the Texas Hill Country. My interest in the fine arts and design only grew throughout the years, and before I knew it, I was attending West Texas A&M University for their Graphic Design program. During my time in the program, I have learned much about how to create and enhance designs to not only draw people in, but to inspire them to become the best version of themselves that they could possibly be. Although I am still an amateur graphic designer, I firmly believe in the importance and value of hard work and determination, and look forward to every opportunity I have to learn something new! I love working with many types of creative people, and always appreciate what ideas others bring to the table during collaborative projects. I enjoy my daily cup of coffee, and love finding inspiration in the everyday beauty of life.
